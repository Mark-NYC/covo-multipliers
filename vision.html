What we mean by covo

Covocational is not just a job description. It is a posture toward mission.

One life, not two

Work and mission are not separate tracks.

Disciple making in ordinary places

We build where we already live, work, and play.

Repeatable rhythms over hype

We want patterns that can actually be lived and passed on.

Who this is for

This is for leaders who:

  • ✓ Have jobs, responsibilities, and real constraints
  • ✓ Want to make disciples without splitting work from mission
  • ✓ Are tired of vague inspiration and want a repeatable path
  • ✓ Want to build alongside others living the same way

What happens if you do not build this on purpose?

You stay busy. You care deeply. But disciple making keeps getting pushed to the edges of life instead of becoming part of its center.

The real problem

Good Intentions Don’t Create Movement

A lot of believers want to make disciples. But they don’t know where to go, what to say, how to follow up, or how ordinary people become churches and leaders.

So they stay busy. They stay sincere. But nothing becomes repeatable.

The rhythm

The 5 Rhythms of a Covo Multiplier

Disciple making breaks down when it stays vague. The 4 Fields gives you a simple pathway. This lab turns that pathway into weekly rhythms you can actually practice.

Go Where are you already sent?

Identify the people and places God has already put in your normal life.

Gospel How do you start spiritual conversations?

Learn simple ways to move from everyday conversation to gospel opportunity.

Grow How do you help someone take next steps?

Build short-term and long-term discipleship rhythms that move people toward obedience.

Gather How do disciples become church?

Learn how to gather new disciples around Scripture, prayer, obedience, and mission.

Guide How do emerging leaders multiply the pattern?

Learn how to guide others to practice the same rhythms with their people.

The problem

The traditional path wasn’t built for modern founders.

Most leadership tracks focus on positions, not platforms. They assume you have all the time, money, and connections. And they rarely prepare you to build a business that funds your mission.

Jobs, not ownership

You are trained to get hired, not to build.

Theory over reps

Too much information. Not enough practice.

Life vs. calling

Faith and work feel separate, not integrated.

Income uncertainty

Good intentions do not pay the bills.

The solution

A sentrepreneur builds businesses that fund mission and freedom.

A sentrepreneur is a founder who builds real businesses in the real world, creating value, generating income, and multiplying impact. You do not need to quit your job. You need a proven pathway, practical training, and a community that goes with you.

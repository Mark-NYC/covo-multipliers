What you’ll take away

What this lab will help you do

  • ✓ Walk away with a clear, reproducible pathway for helping people move from far from God to growing disciple.
  • ✓ Identify the specific conversations and habits that fit naturally into your actual week — without adding a second job.
  • ✓ Leave with something concrete you can start practicing right away, not just ideas to think about later.

Who it’s for

Built for covocational leaders

This is for anyone in a covocational setting — someone who works a regular job, runs a business, or raises a family while trying to make disciples and multiply leaders. No title or seminary background required. Just a desire to live with more intentionality in the places you already are.

About the format

Every lab meets live on Zoom and is intentionally limited to 25 people — small enough for real conversation. Sessions are practical and focused, not lecture-heavy. You’ll leave with something you can use in your actual week.

What happens next

  • Your name — so people know who you are

Already in the WhatsApp? Open the group and post your follow/fish goal: who will you move toward this week?

Why practice matters

The lab gave you a tool. Practice gives you traction. When you use the tool with real people, in real situations, you learn what actually works. You find your rhythm. You build momentum.

Most people don't fail because they lack content — they fail because they try to practice alone. Join the community. Keep showing up. Let others keep you on track.

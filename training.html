The Pathway

One simple pathway, start to finish. Move through it in order.

  1. 0

    Abide

    You cannot multiply what is not alive in you. Start with Jesus. Stay with Jesus. Listen, obey, repent, pray, and move from union with him instead of pressure.

    Tools: Daily Debrief, Prayer Rhythm, Scripture + Obedience

    Abide Training
  2. 1

    Enter

    Your mission field is already around you. Your workplace, neighborhood, gym, dinner table, kids’ school, and regular places. Learn to notice the conversations God is already opening.

    Primary tool: Conversation Box
    Supporting tools: People / Places / Patterns, Prayer Walking, House of Peace

    Enter Training
  3. 2

    Gospel

    Good news has to be spoken. Learn to share your story simply, share God’s story clearly, and ask for a response without making it weird.

    Tools: 15-Second Testimony, 3 Circles, Stories of Hope

    Gospel Training
  4. 3

    Disciple

    New disciples need a path, not just encouragement. Help people hear from Scripture, obey Jesus, share with others, and bring people with them.

    Tools: Three Thirds, Commands of Christ, Discovery Bible Study, “I Will” Statements

    Disciple Training
  5. 4

    Gather

    Disciples were never meant to stay isolated. Gather them into simple church around Scripture, prayer, obedience, care, worship, giving, baptism, communion, and mission.

    Tools: Church Circle, One Another Practice, Baptism + Communion

    Gather Training
  6. 5

    Guide

    Multiplication needs leaders who can reproduce. Train faithful people to take local responsibility for the mission.

    Primary tool: CFC
    Supporting tools: MAWL, 1-3-9 Leadership Pattern, Generational Mapping, Coaching Questions

    Leader Training

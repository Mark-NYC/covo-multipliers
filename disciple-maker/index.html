Disciple Maker Checkup

Discover your next step.

You don't need to master disciple-making before you start. Jesus is inviting you to take one more step, right now, from wherever you are.

This checkup identifies where you currently are on the disciple-making journey and shows you exactly what's next.

What to expect

You'll answer questions about:

  • Your vision — Do you believe in the mission?
  • Your practice — What are you actually doing?
  • Your faithfulness — Are you showing up week after week?
  • Your willingness — Will you let someone sharpen you?
  • Your mission field — Where has God sent you?
  • Your multiplication mindset — Are you thinking beyond yourself?
  • Your dependence on the Spirit — Are you relying on God?
  • Your hunger — Do you actually want this?

Takes about 15–20 minutes. Your progress saves automatically.

After you finish

You'll see a personalized diagnosis of your current journey—not a label or a score, but clarity on what you're strong in and where you're ready to grow.

Then you'll join our WhatsApp community, where we'll connect you with others on this journey, invite you into our online church model, and help you take your next rep.

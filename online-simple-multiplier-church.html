Online Simple Multiplier Church

Online Simple Multiplier Church

A simple online gathering for people who want to join a multiplying DNA church while learning to start one in parallel.

Core commitments

Here is what we are committing to together as we gather.

Vision

Every people and every place reached through multiplying disciples, leaders, and churches.

2 Church Vision Cast

Join a multiplying DNA church and start a church as the training plan.

Core Content

We will work through the waffle: commands, stories, and practices. Use the exact content from obey.tools and the CFC leadership tool.

Follow and Fish Goals

Before each meeting, everyone shares their follow and fish goals in the chat.

Fishing Tool

Everyone commits to using the Conversation Quadrant screenshot as a simple fishing tool and tracking mechanism.

Simple Church Practice

Each person commits to starting a simple church in parallel with the online gathering.

PPPP Focus

Each person identifies their PPPP: passion, people, place, and profession.

CFC Commitment

Each person commits to CFC: commitment, focus, and consistency through the 3-2-1 rhythm.

Shoulder to Shoulder

Each person commits to shoulder-to-shoulder practice. In our case, this means immersion.

FATR Coaching

Multiplier coaching will focus on FATR: faithful, available, teachable, and reproducing.

Meeting Rhythm

  • Thursdays
  • 2 hours
  • 12 weeks, then reassess for continued gathering.

Step 5

Pick one next rep

Not five things. One faithful rep this week.

Casual

  • How long have you been coming here?
  • How’d you get into this?
  • What’s your week been like?

Meaningful

  • What’s been heavy lately?
  • What are you hoping changes this year?
  • What’s something you’re trying to figure out?

Spiritual

  • Do you have any kind of spiritual background?
  • Do you ever pray?
  • Would it be weird if I prayed for that?

Discovery

  • Would you be open to reading a short story about Jesus sometime?
  • I’ve been reading stories of hope with people. Want to try one?
  • Could we look at one passage and just ask what it says?

Build your engagement rhythm

Disciple-making happens inside a real setting, with real people, through a repeatable pattern of showing up. This section helps you name where that can actually happen for you.

Step 1A

Where do you have the most natural access to people right now?

Pick the one lane that's most obvious. This isn't about what you should do — it's about where God has already placed you.

Step 1B

Name the actual setting.

Not "work" or "neighborhood" — be specific. The more concrete, the more useful. Pick a suggestion or type your own.

Step 1C

What kind of oikos exists there — or could?

Oikos means household — the natural web of relationships around you. Check everything that fits.

Step 1D

Design a repeatable rhythm for showing up.

Use what's already in your week. Don't invent margin you don't have.

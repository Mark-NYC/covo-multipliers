The problem

You Have Been in Church. You Have Not Been Trained to Start One.

Most Christians attend church, listen to sermons, join groups, and work through discipleship content. But most have never gathered people around Scripture, prayer, obedience, and mission in a way that looks like church.

The problem is not devotion. It is that most believers have never seen a simple, biblical picture of what church is — or been shown they could gather one.

1 You consume discipleship content

Books, podcasts, courses — without a clear step toward practice.

2 You wait for credentials

A building, a budget, a title, or permission that never comes.

3 You stay stuck in the gap

Between what you learn and what you actually do with others.

The Church Circle

A Simple Map from Acts 2:36–47

The Church Circle is a tool drawn from Acts 2. It names what the first church practiced so that ordinary disciples today can see it clearly, practice it themselves, and gather others around it.

Repent and believe
Be baptized
Receive and obey the Holy Spirit
Abide in the Word of God
Love one another
Practice the Lord's Supper
Pray
Expect signs and wonders
Practice generosity
Worship
Go make disciples
Gather as church
These are not programs. They are practices every believer can do and every gathering can keep — in homes, workplaces, neighborhoods, and relational networks.

The Two-Church Vision Cast

Be in a Church Where You Get Trained. Start a Church Where You Do What You Learn.

This is not about leaving your church. It is about taking what you receive there and practicing it with the people already around you.

A healthy church trains disciples to obey Jesus. Those disciples then gather others into simple expressions of church where they live, work, and play. That is multiplication. That is obedience. That is what Jesus commanded.

You do not need a building, a budget, a stage, or a title. You need Scripture, prayer, obedience, and people.

In 45 minutes, you will have a map and a vision. Enough to know what to do next.

Who can do this

Any Ordinary Disciple of Jesus

Any follower of Jesus can begin gathering people around Scripture, prayer, obedience, love, the Lord's Supper, generosity, worship, and mission. You do not need a building, a budget, a stage, a title, or a professional ministry role. You need to be trained and willing to obey.

A word of honesty

This Is Not for Everyone

This is not for people who want to avoid accountability, start something independent of the body of Christ, or appoint themselves as leaders apart from any oversight. It is not about pretending maturity you do not have or doing your own thing under the cover of "simple church."

It is for ordinary disciples who want to be trained, obey Jesus, gather others, and do it in a simple, biblical, accountable way.

What it is

Not just training. Shared life, real reps, honest debrief.

You are not coming to sit back and collect ideas.

A Covo Immersion is an in-person experience where you step into the real rhythms of co-vocational disciple-makers and simple churches. You do not just sit in a room and hear ideas. You watch the rhythms. You ask questions in the moment. You process what you saw with people actually living it.

The goal is not hype. The goal is clarity. We want people to leave saying, “Now I can actually picture what this could look like where I live, work, and play.”

What makes it different

Most people do not need more ideas. They need sight.

Immersion closes the gap between hearing and seeing.

Most training gives categories, language, and tools. That matters. But a lot of people still leave unsure how it fits into real life. Immersion closes that gap. You see normal settings, normal conversations, normal obstacles, and the actual rhythms that hold the work together.

You are not stepping into a curated highlight reel. You are stepping into a living environment where disciple-making is happening for real.
